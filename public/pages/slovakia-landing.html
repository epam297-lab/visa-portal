NEWS AND UPDATES
15 Jan 2025

New Schengen visa procedures announced

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Slovak Republic has announced streamlined Schengen visa procedures for Kenyan nationals. Applicants are advised to apply at least 15 days before the intended travel date.

05 Mar 2025

Slovakia enhances digital visa services

The Slovak Republic has launched enhanced digital visa application tracking, allowing applicants to monitor their status in real-time through the official portal.

22 Apr 2025

Slovak Embassy Nairobi - New Services

The Embassy of the Slovak Republic in Nairobi has announced extended visa submission hours and improved consular services for applicants from Kenya and neighbouring countries.

FRAUD ALERT

Beware of visa fraud and scams

The Embassy has received reports of fraudulent individuals posing as embassy officials offering visa services for a fee. The Ministry never requests direct payment to personal accounts or mobile money. All official payments are processed through designated banking channels only.

What Your Status Means
1
Application Received
Your application has been received by the Embassy of the Slovak Republic in Nairobi. It is pending initial review and document checklist verification.
2
Document Verification
Your documents are being verified by the consular section. This includes checking the completeness and authenticity of all submitted documents. Additional documents may be requested if needed.
3
Under Review
Your application is under substantive review by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Background checks and eligibility assessment are being carried out. Processing times vary depending on the visa type.
4
Final Outcome
A final decision has been made on your application. You will receive an official notification via email. If approved, further instructions on passport collection or visa issuance will be provided. If refused, you will receive information about appeal options.
