Australian High Commission, Republic of Kenya

The Australian High Commission in Nairobi represents Australia in Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Burundi, Rwanda, Somalia, Seychelles, Ethiopia, South Sudan, Djibouti, and Eritrea.

SCAM WARNING

Be aware of visa scams and fraudulent websites

The Australian High Commission does NOT request payment via mobile money, gift cards, or personal bank transfers. All visa applications must be lodged through the official Department of Home Affairs portal. Report suspicious activity to the High Commission.
